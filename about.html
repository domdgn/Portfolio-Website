About me


Hello! I'm Dominic Duggan, currently in my first year at the University of Winchester, where I'm studying Immersive Technologies.

I have a passion for coding and virtual reality tech, which leads me to spend countless hours prototyping games in Unity, and many more in VR.

On top of that, I love 3D modeling with Blender and Fusion 360, and I'm always pushing myself to improve my skillset in these programs.

I have been using Blender for over 5 years, but my skills have been growing exponentially as my studies progress.

My favourite projects to work on are a mix of fun, challenge, and a bit of experimentation - both for myself and the player - as I find it so rewarding to spend time learning new methodology and logic in my programming, and having it work out after spending hours fixing bugs.

In both my free-time as an indie developer, and my future career, I aim to create engaging and interesting games and art in the digital 3D world.

I'm always on the lookout for exciting new technologies and projects to challenge myself and collaborate with like-minded individuals on!
